KPOP

I have been a big fan of K-pop since 2017. I am interested in K-pop because of its unique combination of music and dancing. Korea Entertainment promotes its artists intelligently. The artists are promoted by using various strategies; one example is making a variety show on the YouTube channel of the group artists. In those shows, it will show the personalities of each member of the group, which will make their fans fall in love with their behavior. This could also be the reason I'm a K-pop fan. There are various groups I have been fangirling with, but for now, I am interested in two groups, which are The Boyz and Riize.
